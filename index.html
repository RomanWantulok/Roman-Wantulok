About Me.

Hi, I'm Roman Wantulok, a 19-year-old automotive photographer and videographer from the Moravian-Silesian region of the Czech Republic. My passion for automobiles has been with me from a young age, and it's something I truly enjoy capturing through my work.

I primarily focus on automotive photography, capturing cars with a Sony A6300 camera and a Sigma 30mm f/1.4 lens to ensure high-quality and detailed shots. For videography, I still use my phone with a professional setup and careful post-production to achieve a cinematic look. I also explore portrait photography and other genres, broadening my creative scope. Every piece of content I produce is enhanced with professional editing software to ensure it stands out.

Automotive photography and videography are more than just a job for me — they're a true passion, and I take great pride in delivering high-quality, visually striking content that truly captures the essence of the automotive world.
